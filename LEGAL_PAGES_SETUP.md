## Key Legal Protections

### Disclaimers
- Service provided "AS IS"
- No verification of tradespeople credentials
- No liability for work quality
- No endorsement of businesses
- Users responsible for their own due diligence

### Limitations of Liability
- Clear separation: You're a directory, not a service provider
- No responsibility for disputes between users and tradespeople
- No liability for damages from services obtained
- Capped liability for indirect damages

### GDPR Compliance
- Clear data collection disclosure
- Legal basis for processing
- User rights outlined
- Data retention policy
- Security measures described
- International transfer provisions
- Right to erasure
- Data portability
