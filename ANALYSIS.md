**Impact:** Low trust - users can't verify quality through peer feedback

**Problem:** Users can only call - no way to request quotes online
**Impact:** Losing customers who prefer digital communication
**Competitor Advantage:** All major competitors offer online quote requests

**Problem:** No way for users to save favorites, track requests, or build history
**Impact:** No user retention or repeat business tracking
**Competitor Advantage:** Competitors build user loyalty through accounts

**Competitor Advantage:** Competitors have rich business profile pages

**Problem:** Can't book appointments online

**Problem:** No immediate help for users with questions
